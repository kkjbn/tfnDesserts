A dessert is a sweet dish enjoyed at the end of a meal. It's meant to be a treat or indulgence and can come in many forms, like cakes, cookies, ice cream, fruit, or pastries. Desserts are often rich and flavorful, providing a pleasant contrast to the savory dishes that precede them. They can be simple, like a piece of chocolate, or more elaborate, like a multi-layered cake.
